F-stop Test

This is an example of what happens when taking the same picture with multiple different F-stop settings. The pictures required changing other settings to maintain a proper exposure. This shows the depth of field go from very narrow, to wide enough to get the entire field in focus.

Shutter Speet Test

The shutter speed test is similar to the F-stop, but instead it shows the affect that changing the shutter speed does on a moving subject. I chose Wynoochee Falls, a large waterfall in the southern Olympics.
