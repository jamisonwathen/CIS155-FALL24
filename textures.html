Gallery Description

This assignment focused on photographing related textures and displaying them in a series. It was fairly open ended. To me this can make things more difficult. I don't see myself as particularly creative, and with something totally open I kind of get choice overload figuring out what to do.

In this case, I focused on textures from suburbia, and textures from my place of work.
