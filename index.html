Gallery Description

This site displays the work done for DMA 136 at Olympic College. The "Quick Assingments" are just that, short and fast paced assignments done while still working on the larger main assignments. They are less refined, but much less time intensive.

The Main assingments are much more intensive, working toward a more refined product. They start simple, showing a basic understanding of camera functions or composition. They work toward a more comprehensive project, bringing together all of the previously learned topics.

I tend to spend a lot of time in the mountains. While some of the projects in the class let me work that love into the class, most did not. To the right is a series of pictures from some of my favorite places in the state. Some are nearby, like Mount Walker. Others are further away, like Mount Adams as seen from Takhkakh Lake.
