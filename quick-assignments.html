DOF Practice

This task was to show as shallow of a depth of field as possible. I used keyboards, as I have several around and everyone is familiar with the scale. I thought this would make it easy to give an instantly recognizable depth of field.

I used a 50mm lens at F1.4. Using this large of an apature created a razor thin depth of field.
